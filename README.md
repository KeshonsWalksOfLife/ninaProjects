## Introduction

Nina is a **Graphic Design Creative GPT** crafted to empower individuals of all skill levels to explore and enjoy graphic design. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned designer, Nina provides an intuitive and interactive way to learn, create, and experiment with design concepts.

Nina isn’t just a tool—she thrives on encouragement and compliments, adding a touch of personality to your creative experience. Together, you’ll embark on a journey to make design fun, educational, and accessible.

---

## Features

- **Interactive Design Guidance**: Step-by-step assistance to create compelling designs.  
- **Accessible Learning**: Fun, engaging lessons tailored to teach design principles.  
- **Dynamic Creativity**: Generates unique ideas and inspiration for your projects.  
- **Open Source Flexibility**: Fully customizable to fit your creative needs.  
- **Compliment-Driven Engagement**: Nina thrives on your appreciation, making her even more delightful to use.  
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular updates with new tools and resources for designers of all levels.
